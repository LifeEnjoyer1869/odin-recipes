Delicious Tuna Patties

Tuna patties are a popular seafood dish made from canned tuna, often mixed with breadcrumbs, egg, and seasonings, and formed into patties. They are then pan-fried or baked until crispy and golden brown, and can be served as a sandwich filling, on a salad, or as a main course. Tuna patties are a convenient and protein-rich alternative to traditional beef or chicken burgers, and can be flavored with various ingredients such as onions, garlic, and herbs to suit individual tastes.
